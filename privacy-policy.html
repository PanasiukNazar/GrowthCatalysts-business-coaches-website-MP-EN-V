Privacy Policy

Introduction

We care about the protection of the privacy and confidentiality of our clients and visitors. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal data in relation to our "Business Coaching" course. By using our website and course, you consent to the practices outlined below.

What Information We Collect

1. Personal Data

While using our course, we may collect your personal data, such as your name, contact details, and other information you choose to provide. These details are collected when you fill out forms, sign up for the course, or contact us via email or other communication channels.

2. Usage Data

We may collect information about how you use our website. This data may include your IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, and the date and time of your visit. This information helps us better understand the needs of our users and improve our materials.

3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and analyze how you use our website. You can manage cookie settings in your browser, but disabling them may affect website functionality.

How We Use Your Information

1. Course Execution

Your personal data is used to deliver the "Business Coaching" course, including responding to inquiries, providing educational materials, and tailoring content to your needs.

2. Communication

We may use your contact details to communicate about course updates, additional materials, and other information related to business coaching. You have the right to opt out of such notifications at any time.

3. Analytics and Research

We use usage data and cookies to analyze trends, manage the website, and improve educational content. This helps us deliver materials better suited to the needs of course participants.

4. Legal Requirements

Your data may be used to fulfill legal requirements, resolve disputes, and enforce our obligations. In case of legal obligations, data may be shared with relevant authorities.

Sharing Your Data

1. Third-Party Services

We may share your personal data with third-party service providers who assist us in managing the website or delivering the course. These providers are obligated to protect your data and cannot use it for other purposes.

2. Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of a business unit, your personal data may be transferred to the new entity. You will be informed, and your rights will remain unaffected.

3. Legal Compliance

We may disclose your personal data if required by law or in response to lawful requests from public authorities.

Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data. However, we cannot guarantee complete security, so we recommend using strong passwords and being cautious when sharing your data.

Your Rights

1. Access and Correction

You have the right to access your personal data and correct it. If you notice any incorrect or incomplete information, please contact us to update it.

2. Deletion

You can request the deletion of your personal data from our systems, provided there are no legal obligations to retain them.

3. Restriction of Processing

You have the right to request the restriction of processing your data in specific circumstances, such as a dispute about the accuracy of the data.

4. Data Portability

You can request a copy of your personal data in a format that allows it to be transferred to another service provider.

5. Objection

You can object to the processing of your personal data for marketing purposes or other legitimate interests. We respect your decisions and will adjust the processing accordingly.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We encourage you to review this page regularly for the latest information.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about the Privacy Policy or how we process your data, please contact us. We are here to help.
